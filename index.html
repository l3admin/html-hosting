The product

A structured compliance automation system, not a chatbot.

Parzley enforces accuracy, traceability and minimises potential for data leakage. It breaks standards into atomic requirements with explicit test instructions, checks a target document against them, then produces findings, recommendations, and remediated outputs.

The core innovation is atomisation: one requirement, one testable condition, one finding.

Common question

Why not just use Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or another off-the-shelf assistant?

Compliance workflows require repeatability, traceability, and controlled evaluation against the same standard every time.

Generic assistants provide no reusable assessment logic, no requirement-level traceability, and no dependable governance workflow.

Parzley transforms policies and standards into reusable assessment infrastructure with evidence-linked findings, remediation workflows, and governed outputs.

A Problem & Use Case specific application of AI

Precision

Policies are decomposed into independently testable requirements instead of broad prompt-based interpretation.

Assessment Logic

Requirements include predefined testing instructions to improve consistency and reduce subjective interpretation.

Repeatability

Reuse the same codified standard across unlimited assessments with consistent evaluation logic.

Auditability

Every finding is linked to specific requirements and supporting evidence for review and traceability.

Visibility

Inspect coverage, partial alignment, missing requirements, and remediation status at requirement level.

Gap Detection

Continuously identify gaps, risk areas, and policy drift before they become audit or operational issues.

Remediation

Generate targeted remediation recommendations and revised documents from identified gaps.

Human Control

Humans remain in control of approvals, edits, findings review, and final document promotion.

Data Handling

Documents are processed in controlled segments to minimise unnecessary data exposure during analysis.

Speed & Throughput

Assessments that normally take hours of manual review can be completed in minutes.

Governance Workflows

Reusable standards, findings, and revised documents persist across repeat governance operations.

Reliability Controls

Multi-stage validation and deterministic checks help improve output consistency and reduce hallucination risk.
